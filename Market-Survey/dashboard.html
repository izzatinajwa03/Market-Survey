MASTER OF SCIENCE IN DATA ANALYTICS

Assalamualaikum and a very good day.

The Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics (FSKM) (previously known as the Faculty of Ocean Engineering Technology and Informatics (FTKKI)), Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is developing a new program, the Master of Science in Data Analytics, which will be implemented using open and distance learning methods, ODL. ODL is the new trend on academic delivery and give chances for those with limited time and space to further their study. The student can study in their own time and their space.

This survey is divided into several parts namely:
Part i: Content Of The Program And Its Relevance To The Needs Of The Organization
Part ii : Career Opportunities
Part iii: Industry Perceptions On Open Distance Learning
Part iv: Others

The results of this survey will be used to support the development of this program. In addition, this survey is also important to improve the quality of graduates so that they are more competitive and relevant to the local and international job market. We would be very pleased if you could provide feedback to this survey. All information obtained is considered confidential. Any questions can be directed to:
